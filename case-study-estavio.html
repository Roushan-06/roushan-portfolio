Project Overview

Estavio Design Studio is a premium home interior design brand in Bangalore. The goal was to create a premium digital presence and a structured lead qualification system for people interested in home interior services.

In the interior design industry, many enquiries come from people who are casually exploring, do not have budget clarity, do not have possession of the property, or are not ready to start soon. A basic enquiry form does not solve this problem.

This was built as more than a website. It was built as a lead qualification funnel.

The funnel helps the business capture better project details before the sales team starts follow-up.

The Problem

Lack of structured qualification: a normal enquiry form only collects name and phone number.

Low-context enquiries: interior leads need location, property type, budget, possession status, and timeline.

Sales team time wastage: without qualification, teams may spend time with people who are not ready or not budget-fit.

Need for premium positioning: the site needed to feel clean, professional, and trustworthy.

Need for follow-up: capturing leads was not enough without a clear nurture process.

Project Goal

  • • Present Estavio as a premium interior design brand
  • • Capture serious home interior enquiries
  • • Collect useful project details before the first sales call
  • • Improve lead quality and reduce irrelevant enquiries
  • • Create a clearer CRM and follow-up process
  • • Support paid ad campaigns with a conversion-focused landing experience

My Strategy

I planned the project around one main idea: do not collect only contact details, collect buying context. For interior design businesses, lead quality depends heavily on the prospect's project readiness.

What I Built

1. Premium Website Structure

I created the website structure to present Estavio as a premium interior design brand with clean, professional, conversion-focused positioning.

2. Lead Qualification Funnel

The funnel collected useful project details so the business could identify serious, budget-ready, location-fit, timeline-ready users.

3. Budget-Based Enquiry Flow

The budget-based approach helped prospects think clearly about their project and gave the business better information before follow-up.

4. CRM Lead Flow

Submitted enquiries were structured for CRM tracking across new enquiries, qualified leads, follow-up status, conversations, and next actions.

5. Follow-Up System

The follow-up structure used WhatsApp, email, and SMS messaging around project planning, budget clarity, consultation, timeline readiness, trust-building, and next steps.

Why It Was Better Than a Basic Website

A basic website only shows information. This project was planned as a system: a premium digital presence, enquiry capture engine, qualification layer, CRM process, and follow-up structure.

The website was not only built to look good. It was built to support lead generation and sales follow-up.
