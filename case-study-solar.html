Project Overview

SKIG Energies needed a better way to generate genuine solar enquiries. Before this funnel, the client had tested multiple marketing approaches but was receiving many low-quality leads. Some people filled forms casually, some submitted by mistake, and many enquiries were not serious enough for the sales team to spend time on.

The main goal was not just to generate more leads. The goal was to generate better-qualified solar leads by making prospects share useful information before reaching the sales team.

Calculate Your Solar Budget in 30 Seconds.

The live page clearly positions the calculator for homeowners with monthly electricity bills of Rs. 3,000 or more and asks for important qualification details before showing an estimate.

The Problem

Junk leads from basic forms: people were submitting forms without real buying intent.

Low-quality enquiries: the sales team did not have enough information to understand whether the person was actually fit for solar installation.

Manual qualification burden: the team had to spend time calling leads who were not serious, not eligible, or did not have the right property conditions.

My Strategy

Instead of creating another simple lead form, I planned a qualification-first funnel where users had to take a meaningful action before becoming a lead.

  • • Monthly electricity bill
  • • Property type and ownership confirmation
  • • Rooftop availability and roof shadow condition
  • • Contact details, address, area, and pincode
  • • Preferred language

What I Built

1. Calculator-Based Landing Page

I created a landing page that gave users a clear reason to submit their details: a solar budget estimate based on their inputs.

2. Lead Qualification Structure

The funnel filtered users based on important solar-fit factors like ownership and rooftop availability.

3. Zoho Pipeline Integration

After form submission, lead data was connected to the client's Zoho pipeline for structured lead management.

4. Follow-Up System

Automated WhatsApp, email, and SMS follow-ups supported leads after submission and improved sales handoff.

Why It Worked Better

A basic lead form only collects name and phone number. This funnel collected buying context: bill size, ownership, rooftop space, roof suitability, location, and follow-up priority.

Result

  • • Reduced irrelevant enquiries
  • • Improved lead quality
  • • Created a clearer sales follow-up process
  • • Connected lead data directly into Zoho pipeline
  • • Helped the sales team focus on serious prospects
  • • Created a scalable funnel for paid campaigns
