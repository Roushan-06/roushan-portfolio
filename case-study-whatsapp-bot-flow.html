Project Overview

This project was built for a furniture company that needed a better way to qualify incoming WhatsApp leads before the sales team got involved.

The business was receiving enquiries, but not every lead was serious or sales-ready. Many users asked basic questions, dropped off midway, or did not provide enough information for the team to understand their requirement.

I created a WhatsApp bot flow that automatically qualifies leads based on their responses and guides them through a structured conversation.

The goal was to reduce manual qualification work and help the sales team focus only on better-quality prospects.

The Problem

Too many unqualified enquiries: leads were coming in, but the team had to manually understand every requirement.

Slow response time: manual replies caused delays when multiple enquiries arrived at the same time.

Missing requirement details: the sales team often did not know what the customer wanted, their budget, or how serious they were.

Manual lead filtering: the team had to spend time asking the same basic questions repeatedly.

Lead leakage: some prospects dropped off because they did not receive quick, structured responses.

Project Goal

  • • Respond instantly to new enquiries
  • • Ask the right qualification questions
  • • Understand customer requirements
  • • Filter serious and non-serious leads
  • • Collect important sales information
  • • Reduce manual work for the team
  • • Move qualified leads toward the next step

Follow-Up / Next Step

What I Built

1. WhatsApp Bot Conversation Flow

I created a structured WhatsApp conversation flow that guides users step by step with clear questions instead of open-ended manual replies.

2. Lead Qualification Logic

The flow qualified leads based on product interest, furniture requirement, budget range, location, timeline, contact details, and buying intent.

3. Automated Lead Filtering

Qualified leads could move forward for human follow-up, while low-intent or incomplete leads could be handled differently.

4. Sales Handoff Structure

The bot collected the right information before handoff, so the sales team received better context before contacting the prospect.

5. Reduced Manual Repetition

The team no longer had to ask the same basic questions again and again because the bot collected key information automatically.

Why This Worked Better

A normal WhatsApp inbox creates chaos when enquiries increase. This bot flow converted WhatsApp into a structured lead qualification system.

The system made WhatsApp work like a proper sales qualification channel instead of just a chat inbox.
