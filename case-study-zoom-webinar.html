Zoom Webinar Funnel - CRM Automation & Nurture System

A live webinar operations system built to track leads, manage pipeline stages, trigger nurture messages, and segment attendees based on Zoom attendance data.

Project Overview

This project was built for a CRM and product-based company running webinars to generate qualified product enquiries and sales conversations.

My role focused on the automation, CRM, lead tracking, and nurture messaging side of the webinar funnel. The main goal was to make sure webinar leads were properly tracked, segmented, reminded, and followed up based on their stage in the funnel.

The system helped manage the journey from registration to qualification, attendance tracking, post-webinar follow-up, and sales pipeline movement.

I was not responsible for the complete webinar strategy, webinar content, or live webinar delivery. My contribution was focused on making the automation, CRM, and nurture system work properly.

The Problem

Lead tracking was not structured enough: registrations, qualified leads, attendees, non-attendees, and follow-up leads needed to be managed clearly inside the CRM.

Manual follow-up created confusion: the team had to manually check lead status and decide who needed reminders or follow-up.

Attendance-based follow-up was needed: people who attended and people who missed the webinar needed different communication.

Nurture messaging had to be consistent: WhatsApp and email reminders needed to go out at the right time before and after the webinar.

Sales needed better visibility: the team needed to know which leads were registered, contacted, qualified, attended, missed, and ready for follow-up.

Project Goal

  • • Track leads across the webinar journey
  • • Organize leads inside the CRM pipeline
  • • Trigger reminders and nurture messages
  • • Segment attendees and non-attendees
  • • Support post-webinar follow-up
  • • Reduce manual tracking work
  • • Give the sales team clearer lead visibility

Automation Flow

Webinar Registration
Lead Created / Updated in CRM
Lead Moved to Registration Stage
Reminder & Nurture Messages Triggered
Telecaller / Team Qualification Update
Qualified Lead Moved to Next Stage
Zoom Attendance Data Checked
Attended / Missed Segmentation
Post-Webinar Follow-Up Triggered
Sales Pipeline Updated

What I Managed

1. CRM Pipeline Setup

I helped structure the CRM pipeline so leads could be tracked clearly across registered, contacted, qualified, reminder sent, attended, missed, follow-up required, interested, and demo booked stages.

2. Automation Workflow Setup

I worked on workflows that moved leads between stages and triggered communication based on lead status, reducing manual work and making follow-up more consistent.

3. Nurture Messaging

I created and managed WhatsApp and email messages for confirmations, reminders, WhatsApp group updates, value messages, joining links, missed webinar follow-up, and demo follow-up.

4. Zoom Attendance-Based Follow-Up

I supported the attendance tracking process so attendees and non-attendees could receive different follow-up paths based on their webinar behavior.

5. Sales Team Visibility

The CRM and automation setup helped the team understand who registered, who was contacted, who was qualified, who attended, who missed, who needed follow-up, and who booked a call.
